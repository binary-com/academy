Different Types of Trade

Rise/Fall

Trade on whether a market will RISE or FALL from its current spot price.

Higher/Lower

Trade on whether a market will finish HIGHER or LOWER than a predetermined price target.

Touch/No Touch

Trade on whether a market will TOUCH or NOT TOUCH a predetermined price target anytime during the contract period.

In/Out

Trade on whether a market will STAY BETWEEN or GO OUTSIDE two price targets.
