Paul Wallace spent six years in the Royal Air Force controlling fighter jets before embarking upon a career in the City. He started trading during the dot-com boom of the late 90’s and then went full-time in 2003. He has traded both as a private trader and as head trader for funds. Through his work at Tradingbeliefs.com, a performance support practice for traders, he has been helping improve trader performance for 9 years and has worked with traders from Tier 1 Banks, Hedge Funds, Prop Shops and HNWI clients.

For the last 6 years he has run Europe’s largest independent Trader social events: The London Traders Network.

His trades, market commentary and educational pieces are posted on his blog, FXTraderPaul.com, and on twitter @FXTraderPaul.
