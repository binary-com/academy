Advice From Thomas

Forecasting market movements is not a science that can be purely derived through mathematical means. Chaotic elements are also present in the ultimate market movement equation such as – earthquakes, tsunamis, accidents, wars, political events, release of economic data, political statements. Moreover, fear and greed are the dominant emotions driving markets with no mathematical yardsticks to measure the amounts at play, nor existing logic behind them. However, such sentiment must also be accounted for. While part of the answer may be found in technical charting, it’s the rest of the answer that’s elusive and that is exactly what MarketsAndYou focuses on.
