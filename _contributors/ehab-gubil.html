Dr. Ehab Gubil is an investing trainer with extensive knowledge in financial trading. Born in Egypt, Dr. Ehab finished his studies in medicine and also managed to set up his own medical practice. During this time, he had a new-found interest in financial trading and investment, and decided to pursue this interest of his by taking up further studies in finance and economics.

Gradually, he started sharing his knowledge with his friends and he realized that even those with very little education in finance were able to understand him and achieve positive trading results. As a result, he believed that larger crowds could benefit from his training and experience. It was during this time that he decided to leave his medical practice behind and to start conducting seminars in investment and financial trading to residents of Saudi Arabia and the UAE with occasional seminars in Cairo, Egypt.

Dr. Ehab stays in touch with many of his students through his website, EG-academy, where he provides online webinars and many tips on how to trade successfully. He also provides a signal service to his students and subscribers. He prides himself in having a huge amount of patience and derives great satisfaction from the positive results achieved by his students/subscribers whether at an introductory or advanced level.
