Vernon Lees is a financial trader and writer who has considerable experience in both market analysis and strategy development. With a background in IT and product development, he quit his full time job to take up trading full time in 2007.

Over the last six years he has successfully developed and traded a number of financial strategies, focusing on a number of different markets.

He is a keen trader of stocks, indices and the foreign exchange markets, making use of both technical and fundamental approaches to identify and validate trading opportunities. Vernon was first introduced to binary options trading in 2006 and since then, he has been using the Binary.com trading platform as part of his core investment strategy.

He is also an author who regularly writes ebooks to share his trading knowledge and experience, as well as to provide trading signals, such as the ebook he published in January 2015 entitled “Binary Options Trading System In A Diary 2015.” Vernon has been a collaborator with Binary.com for many years and he generally offers his ebooks free of charge exclusively to Binary.com clients.
