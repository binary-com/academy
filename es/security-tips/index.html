Compartir

Here at Binary.com we take that extra step to protect you. Here are a few guidelines to secure your data and to stay safe online

Don't click links in emails, SMSes, or pop-ups. Always type the web address yourself. The correct URL is www.binary.com

Never share information such as your Username, email address and password via emails, texts or phone calls.

Never respond to unsolicited promotional emails. If in doubt, just delete the email.

Check out the padlock icon on your browser. This icon indicates that the website uses a secure connection.

Always enable two-factor authentication (2FA) for your account. Follow these steps to enable 2FA now.

Invest in computer security such as a personal firewall, anti-spyware, or anti-virus software. Update this software regularly!
